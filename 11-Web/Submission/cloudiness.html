Summary: Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Plot shows weak, non linear associaton between city cloudiness % and latitudes. There does not seem to be trend between cloudiness and latitude. However, there are concentrations of cities with 100% cloudiness between latitues -20 to 0 and 40 to 70. There are also concentrations for 0 cloudines between latitudes 10 to 40.
